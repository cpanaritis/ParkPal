    Frequently Asked Questions How can I save my location into my Favorites?

    Enter in the location and click on the star icon beside the location

    How can I remove location from Favorites?

    Click on either the location or destination text fields to allow for favorites list to drop down. Click remove on the desired location to remove it from favorites.

    How do I use the Night Mode display?

    Simply click on night mode icon located on the top left

    How can I change distance from kilometers to miles?

    Click on the toggle button to change between kilometers and miles

    How can I see me all available parking station?

    Simply click on the “List Parking Area” button
